INTRODUCING THE BRAND NEW JAM: MY WOMAN

Winky Dee does it again

It has been quite some time since the gaffer hit maker left the music spotlight last year. This year, He comes with something unique and fresh. Featuring the multi-raggae award winner, Bin Manie, Winky releases a track titled "My Woman". In the song, He urges His lover on how she always fails to love him genuinely despite problems they face.
